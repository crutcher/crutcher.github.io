# bimm - burn image models library

[bimm][bimm.repo] is my ongoing effort to expand the scope of
[burn][burn.repo] by adding support for image classification models
equivalent to those in [timm][timm.repo].

[bimm.repo]: https://github.com/crutcher/bimm
[burn.repo]: https://crates.io/crates/burn
[timm.repo]: https://github.com/huggingface/pytorch-image-models
